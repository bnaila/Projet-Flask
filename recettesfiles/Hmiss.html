Hmiss

Ingredients

2 green peppers
2 red bell pepper
1 tomato
3 cloves of garlic
Olive oil
salt pepper

Preparation

Roast the peppers and tomato in the oven or on the barbecue.
Remove from the oven and place the peppers and tomato in a bag (the skin will be easier to remove).
Remove the skin and seeds and let drain in a colander. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan, brown the minced garlic, add the tomato cut into small pieces.
Leave on the heat for a few minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the peppers cut into small pieces, salt and pepper.
Simmer for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve on small plates, drizzling with olive oil.
