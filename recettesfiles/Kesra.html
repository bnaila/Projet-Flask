Kesra

Ingredients

1 kg of fine semolina
1 tea glass of oil
Salt (it all depends on everyone's taste)
Water sufficient quantit

Preparation

Pouring the oil on your semolina, and sand between your hands to make the oil absorb.
Add the water little by little until you get a paste that is neither too hard nor sticky.
A smooth paste. I let rest 20 minutes. Heat an anti-adhesive pan or a special tajine kesra.
Place the dough on a cloth or work surface. Spread with the palm of the hand and then with a rolling pin (it depends on the thickness you prefer, I prefer it thin) Smooth the surface and prick with a fork.
Carefully place the cake on the pan and let it cook on one side before turning it over. The cake is ready when it is cooked and golden on both sides.
