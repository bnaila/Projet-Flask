Karantika (chickpea flan)

Ingredients

250g chickpea flour
1 glass of milk
1 glass of oil
1L water
1 egg
3 portions of cottage cheese
1 teaspoon of salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/4 teaspoon Cumin

Preparation

In a receptacle put the chickpea flour, add the egg and the cheese.
Using a mixer or a whisk mix until the mixture becomes pasty.
add the glass of oil mix well then add the milk then the water let stand 10min. Then mix well before pouring into a 20cm/25cm deep dish.
baked 180Â° rotating heat for about 1 hour.
Serve in slices with a spicy sauce (harissa) sprinkle some cumin
