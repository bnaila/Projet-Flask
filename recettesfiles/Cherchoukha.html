Chekhchoukha

Ingredients

2 red peppers
1 green bell pepper
2 tomatoes
2 eggs
2 cloves of garlic
1 tsp. paprika
1 bunch of parsley
salt and pepper

Preparation

Wash the peppers and place them directly on an oven rack.
Bake for about 25 to 30 minutes. the peppers should blacken on all surfaces. you can also grill them on a barbecue.
Chop the garlic cloves. Chop the parsley. As soon as the skin starts to blister, take them out of the oven, place them in a bowl, cover with cling film and leave to rest for 15 minutes.
Place the peppers on a cutting board and remove the skin. Cut the peppers in half, remove the seeds and cut into julienne strips.
Bring a pot of water to a boil and immerse the tomatoes in it for one minute. Drain them, remove the skin and cut them into julienne strips. Heat a frying pan with a little olive oil and fry the garlic for a few minutes before adding the tomatoes, then the peppers. Continue cooking for 5 minutes.Sprinkle with paprika, season with salt and pepper and cook until the peppers are melting. Break the eggs into the pan and cook like fried eggs over the filling. Sprinkle with parsley before serving hot.
